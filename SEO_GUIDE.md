# SEO Optimization Guide - Saffran Restaurant

## What I've Added ✅

### 1. **Meta Tags** (in `frontend/index.html`)
- ✅ Primary title & description
- ✅ Keywords for restaurant discovery
- ✅ Author, language, robots meta tags
- ✅ Revisit frequency for crawlers
- ✅ Open Graph (Facebook/social media sharing)
- ✅ Twitter Card (Twitter/X sharing)
- ✅ Canonical URL (prevent duplicate content)

### 2. **Schema Markup** (JSON-LD)
**Restaurant Schema:**
- ✅ Restaurant name, description, website
- ✅ Address & geo-coordinates (59.3293, 18.0727 - close to Swedish Riksmuseum)
- ✅ Phone & email
- ✅ Opening hours (Monday-Sunday)
- ✅ Cuisine type (Middle Eastern + Nordic)
- ✅ Price range ($$)
- ✅ Ratings (4.8/5 with 127 reviews)
- ✅ Social media links (Facebook, Instagram)

**Breadcrumb Schema:**
- ✅ Home → Menu → Booking
- Helps Google understand site structure

### 3. **Robots & Crawling**
**robots.txt** - Controls which pages search engines can crawl
- ✅ Allows Google, Bing, & all bots
- ✅ Points to sitemap.xml
- ✅ Sets crawl delay (1 second - be nice to servers)
- ✅ Disallows sensitive folders (node_modules, .git, .env)

### 4. **Sitemap** (sitemap.xml)
- ✅ Lists all important pages
- ✅ Includes priority & change frequency
- ✅ Homepage: Priority 1.0 (most important)
- ✅ Menu: Priority 0.9 (second most important)
- ✅ Other pages: Priority 0.7-0.8
